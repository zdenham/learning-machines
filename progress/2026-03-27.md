### 📝 Q1: Law of Cosines

**Problem:**
Given a triangle with sides a, b, c and angle θ between a and b, place it in coordinates and derive:

c² = a² + b² − 2ab cos(θ)

**Hint:** Put one side on the x-axis, express the third point using (b cos θ, b sin θ), apply distance formula.

### 📝 Q2: Dot Product Identity

**Problem:**
For **u**, **v** ∈ ℝⁿ, show:

**u** · **v** = ‖**u**‖ ‖**v**‖ cos(θ)

**Hint:** Expand ‖**u** − **v**‖² algebraically and via law of cosines, then equate.
